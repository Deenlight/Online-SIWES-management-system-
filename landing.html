About SIWES

The Students Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) is a skill acquisition program designed for Nigerian students in tertiary institutions. It bridges the gap between theoretical classroom learning and practical industrial experience by placing students in real-world work environments.

Learn More

Our SIWES E-Logbook system is a digital solution that simplifies the process of recording and managing industrial work experience. Students can submit their daily/weekly logs, while supervisors can review and provide feedback seamlessly.
